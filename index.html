About

Our school was established in 2003 in Buner with the aim of providing quality education and strong moral values. Since its establishment, the school has been working sincerely to build a bright future for students through discipline, dedication, and academic excellence.

Achievements

Our school has achieved excellent results in the Swat Board examinations. Students consistently perform well, reflecting the hard work of our teachers and the commitment of the institution to academic success.

Activities

Along with academics, the school actively promotes co-curricular activities. We regularly organize Sports Gala, Iqbal Day celebrations, quiz competitions, and other educational activities to build confidence, teamwork, and leadership skills among students.

Girls Section

The Girls Section has a beautiful and well-maintained building with a well-equipped laboratory and activity room. A safe, supportive, and learning-friendly environment is provided to help students grow academically and personally.

Boys Section

The Boys Section offers a spacious building with modern facilities, including a well-equipped lab and activity area. The section focuses on quality education, discipline, and the overall development of students.
